A smart inventory management and spoilage prediction system for small and medium retail stores.

## Overview  
EcoShelf empowers kirana stores and similar retailers to stay on top of their inventory by predicting which items are at risk of expiring soon. With an intuitive dashboard and timely alerts, store owners can organize shelves better, reduce waste, and minimize losses â€” all with minimal technical effort.

## The Problem  
Small and medium retail stores often face:
- Difficulty keeping track of expiration dates  
- Losses due to unsold or spoiled products  
- Manual, inefficient shelf arrangements  
- No clear system for inventory risk prioritization  

## Our Solution  
EcoShelf simplifies this with:
- A machine learning-based spoilage risk predictor  
- Real-time inventory dashboard with visual cues  
- Smart alerts for critical items  
- Shelf management tools to rearrange stock based on risk  
- A clean, interactive interface designed for everyday use
